ABOUT

About Liquid Opinions

Liquid Opinions is a user-friendly application for desktop and mobile devices that allows you to join a research panel and earn rewards. As a panel member you will have the opportunity to take part in online surveys, face-to-face and online focus groups for which you will be awarded points that can be redeemed for cash.

PROCESS

The registration process

Upon registering you will be sent an email confirmation. Then you will have the option to take part in a profiling survey to provide us more information about yourself so we can send you tailored surveys.
