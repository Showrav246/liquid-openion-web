The points system is very basic and easy to understand. 1 point equals 1 penny, so 50 points will be 50 pence and 300 points will be £3.

Each time we send you an email invitation to take part in a survey it will contain details of the points available for completion.

To see how much money you have earned to-date, please visit our website: www.liquidopinions.com, log-in to your account and view the “earn points” section.

When you have reached 2000 points, which is equivalent to £20, we make your cash incentive payment via PayPal.

You will not receive ANY SPAM as a result of joining our panel. Liquid Opinions is obliged to the Market Research Society Code of Conduct and Data protection Act 1998, which means no data will be passed on to other parties unless you have given permission.

All personal information you provide to Liquid Opinions is confidential and will not be shared unless it is specifically stated otherwise. Liquid Opinions never links survey answers to individual panel members, so you will remain anonymous unless it is specifically stated otherwise.

If you are selected to take part in a survey hosted by a partner then all information you provide will be confidential and you will be anonymous.
